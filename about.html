Our Story

Building Excellence Since 2017

Maberx Ltd is a fast-growing steel fabrication firm that specializes in the design, fabrication, and installation of all types of structural steel works. Our works range in types and size including industrial, commercial, religious, institutional, governmental, and private clients.

The company was established in 2017 and has, ever since, expanded progressively to combine experienced and highly skilled personnel with a successful history of undertaking and delivering projects.

Our Vision

To be the leading steel fabrication and engineering solutions provider in East Africa.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality, sustainable, and innovative steel solutions that exceed client expectations.
